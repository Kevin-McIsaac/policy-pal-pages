1. About this policy


1.1 Why this policy?

This policy will help you identify a borrower’s eligibility for a Sustainable Upgrades Home Loan (SUHL) or Sustainable Upgrades Investment Loan (SUIL).
This offering is available for applications assessed via OBP only, it is not available to applications assessed via TLA, RAP or BLAST.


1.2 What is a Sustainable Upgrades Home or Investment Loan

A Sustainable Upgrades Home or Investment Loan is for the purpose of upgrading a borrower’s owner occupied and/or investment property, to achieve energy efficiencies and/or implement climate resilience measures to protect their property against natural disasters. 


2. Rules you must apply 


2.1 Overview

This policy outlines the borrower eligibility requirements for accessing a SUHL or SUIL. Where not otherwise detailed below, all other prevailing standard non-mortgage insured policies and product offerings for fully documented loans apply.
This policy is to be read in conjunction with the policies and procedures contained in other sections of the Consumer Mortgage Credit Policy Manual

2.3 Eligible Sustainable Upgrades

For a list of allowable energy efficient and climate resilient upgrades that meet the requirements for an eligible Sustainable Upgrades home or investment loan, refer to Westpac Eligible Upgrades website to see the criteria (effective 26th August).


2.4 Progress Payments

In all cases, drawdown is via progress payments only and on receipt of an invoice either at settlement or during the progress payment period. Payment will be made directly to the supplier / installer, excluding any deposit paid by the borrower, which can be reimbursed where evidence held to support.
Full loan proceeds must be drawn within 9 months of the date of the loan offer. Where the loan has not been fully drawn within 9 months, the loan will be converted to principal and interest, and any undrawn funds will not accessible.

3. Process to follow

Applications can be submitted in ApplyOnline (AOL) from the 26th August 2024.
The Sustainable Upgrades Home/Investment Loan is included in the Product Selector stage of AOL.
AOL will display the cost categories related to Sustainable Upgrades Loans for Brokers to record how the funds will be utilised and will trigger validations to prevent applications from proceeding without the valid data captured.
