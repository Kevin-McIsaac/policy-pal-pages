Minimum Standards to Submit a Home Loan to Commonwealth Bank

Back to Submission Quality

To ensure your applications are processed as smoothly as possible, our processing teams will not commence the assessment process unless the following items as a minimum are sent to the Bank:

  • Applicant/s privacy consent - via the Home Loan On-Boarding Application 002-829
  • Satisfactory Identification must be provided - with the Home Loan On-Boarding Application 002-829
  • Home Loan Supplementary Details Guarantor form (002-832) (If applicable)

 

Applications submitted without these items will not progress. A notification will be returned to the Broker using the standard method.
