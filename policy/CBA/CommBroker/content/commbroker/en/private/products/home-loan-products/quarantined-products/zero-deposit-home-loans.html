The $0 Deposit Home Loan is designed to assist Australians purchase their first home sooner

Features and Benefits

 

Loan Purpose Features The $0 Deposit Home Loan is available on: The $0 Deposit Home Loan is not available on:

  • Construction of dwelling;

  • Purchase of established dwelling; and

  • Purchase of newly erected dwelling.

 

Not available for Investment loans or refinancing

  • First time buyers can borrow up to 100% of the property value (inclusive of LMI premium and fees);

  • Loan purpose must be related to the purchase/ construction of residential property;

  • Maximum loan amount is $500,000;

  • No refund of LMI Premium is payable by Genworth

  • Standard Variable Rate (SVR) Home Loan;

  • 12 Month Discounted Variable Rate Home Loan;

  • 1 year Guaranteed Rate Home Loan;

  • 1-5, 7, 10 and 15 Year Fixed Rate Home Loan; and

  • 3 Year Rate Saver and Rate Saver Home Loans (Base Variable Rate).

  • Any investment loan;

  • Line of Credit; and

  • Equity Unlock for Seniors.

 

 

Lenders Mortgage Insurance Requirements Eligibility Criteria Lending Margins

  • All $0 Deposit Home Loans must be approved and insured with the bank preferred Mortgage Insurer;

  • Maximum loan size of 500K;

  • LMI premium is payable by the customer.

All borrowers must be eligible for the First Home Owners Grant Scheme (FHOGS)

Up to 100% - Please note, due to the manual processes in place it is not possible to capitalise the LMI Premium into the loan. Conditions apply.
