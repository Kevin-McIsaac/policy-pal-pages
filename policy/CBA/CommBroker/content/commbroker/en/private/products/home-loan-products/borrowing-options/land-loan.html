Land Loan

Description

A land loan is designed to assist customers with the purchase of vacant land to build an owner occupied home or residential investment property.

 

The features are as follows:

  • provides the flexibility to purchase land and build a home at a later date
  • customers can apply for a loan to build a home before the land loan is repaid in full
  • available for owner occupied or investment purposes

 

Loan Options

  • Standard Variable Rate
  • Fixed Rate
  • Extra Variable Rate 

Note:

  • The Bank's usual Terms and Conditions apply to land loans, including the standard home loan interest rate.
  • Land loans are not eligible for LMI/LPD waivers
  • Vacant land size greater than 11 hectares is unacceptable security for loans with Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI)/Low Deposit Premium (LDP)
  • For unregistered land, loan approval is conditional subject to the following:
    • A satisfactory on completion valuation must be conducted when the Valuer can identify and physically inspect the proposed allotment by accessing the land. Where access to the land is not available, the application must be treated as a Home Seeker.
    • If valuation is not accepted within 90 days of application approval, the customer's financial details must be reconfirmed.
    • If there are any changes to the security position and / or the repayment capacity, the approval may be withdrawn.
    • For applications involving LMI, conditional approval is extended to six months only. LMI approval lapses after the six months. After this time a new application must be completed.
    • For LMI premium calculation purposes, the 'on completion' valuation will be accepted. For contracts entered into at least six months before the valuation date, the 'on completion' valuation will be accepted for premium calculation purposes only.
