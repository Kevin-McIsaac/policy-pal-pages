Repayment Reducer - Closed 29 June 2023

                                                  Back to Quarantined Home Loan Products

The Repayment Reducer (RR) feature is closed as of 29 June 2023. Impacted customers with open RR home loans have been compensated and will have a copy of the mandatory letter imaged to their home loan account.

If a customer with Repayment Reducer wishes to make changes to their loan (including, but not limited to switching product), the RR discount will cease to apply.

If you or your customer has any queries relating to RR, please reach out to your Relationship Manager.

Repayment Reducer allows the customer to obtain a discount on their interest rate by paying a one-off non-refundable fee. This discount remains on the loan until it is repaid.

 

Interest rate:

The discount applied was dependant on the type of loan the customer selected.

referring to the following table to determine the product type available and the discount applied.

Product Type Interest Rate Discount
Economiser Home Loan/Investment Home Loan (excludes 3 Year Special Economiser) 0.30% p.a. (Commonwealth Bank Branded only)
Rate Saver Home Loan/Investment Home Loan (excludes 3 Year Special Rate Saver) 0.30% p.a. ( Colonial Branded only)

 

Fees:

  • An application Fee based on the fixed percentage of the loan amount payable, less any Bank Fees applicable.
  • All fees were charged to the loan account or could be paid up front by a deposit being made to the loan account prior to funding.
  • Switching fee/Application fee was not refundable once the loan has been funded/switched.
