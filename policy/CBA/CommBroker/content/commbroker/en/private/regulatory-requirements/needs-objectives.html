Needs & Objectives interview guide (N&O)

Back to Regulatory Requirements

 

A Needs & Objectives (N&O) interview guide will be generated via the completion of the 'Compliance' tab in ApplyOnline / CommApply as part of the home loan application process.

The Needs & Objectives (N&O) interview guide captures vital customer information as part of the home loan process and is to be completed during the conversation with the customer, before the application is submitted.

The guide ensures we are asking customers the right questions to understand their financial situation and offer the best product to suit their needs.

We are making this change as part of our continued commitment to our responsible lending and regulatory obligations. 

Once the application is submitted the completed N&O guide will automatically be sent to the bank.

 

What you need to do

Complete the new Compliance tab N&O Interview Guide for all new applications as well as amendments to applications in progress in ApplyOnline/ CommApply.

Review and capture the reasons the applicant(s) has selected a particular product type, feature and repayment type. You will also be required to document the retirement conversation outcome.

 

What you need to Know

Top Ups, VLOC increases and switching are out of scope.

Once the 'Compliance tab has been completed N&O interview guide can be printed from the document pack print tool (optional).

The N&O interview guide will automatically be sent to the bank once the Compliance tab is completed and the application is submitted. 

The interview guide is valid for the life of the application.
