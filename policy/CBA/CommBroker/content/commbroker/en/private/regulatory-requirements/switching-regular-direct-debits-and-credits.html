Switching regular direct debits and credits from an OFI

Back to Regulatory Requirements

From 1 July 2012, switching transaction accounts from one financial institution to another became a whole lot easier.

The banking industry has implemented assisted switching services that make it easier for customers to switch their regular payments to their new account with their new bank. 

Under this arrangement CommBank can:

  • Obtain a list from the customers' previous Financial Institution (OFI) of the regular payments on the OFI account over the past 13 months and send it to the customer to review. This process can take up to 13 business days to complete.
  • If required by the customer, contact the organisations making payments to or from the customer's account and request them to update the customers records with the customer's new account details. This process can take up to an additional 15 business days to complete.

To find out more, visit the How to switch page on CommBank and review the frequently asked questions.
