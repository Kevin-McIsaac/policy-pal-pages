Corporations Act (FSR)

What is a financial service and what is a financial product?

Back to Corporations Act

Financial service is:

  • Providing financial product advice; or
  • Dealing in a financial product (that is issuing or establishing, varying or disposing of a financial product).

 

Financial product advice is any recommendation or statements of opinion, or a report of either of those things, that is likely to influence a person's decision in relation to a financial product, or which could reasonably be regarded as being intended to have such an influence.
