Customer Repayment Set Up

 

 

The bank has introduced changes to our process to avoid customers missing their first payment as a result of a new repayment account not being opened prior to settlement.

 

When a customer requests a new account opening for the purpose of making their repayments, please refer to the following steps when submitting your documents:

 

Step Process
1 Complete all relevant sections of the Home Loan On-Boarding Application form 002-829 (including Part B and/or Part D);
2 Note the Third Party Application Checklist 001-286 accordingly
3

Send the Home Loan On-Boarding Application form 002-829 to the Bank with all other supporting documents.

  • For detailed instructions on emailing or faxing documents, please refer to Sending Documents to the Bank

 

Weekly/Fortnightly Home Loan Repayments

When you discuss the repayment cycle and frequency with your customer, you must remind them that the first loan repayment can commence from the following business day after funding as per the customer/s choice of day and frequency e.g. if the customer chooses to make weekly repayments on a Friday and we fund their loan on a Thursday, the first repayment will be made the following day. This will ensure the loan does not go into partial arrears in the first month. Alternatively, you may ask us to delay commencement of the first weekly/fortnightly repayment until after receipt of the first monthly repayment.

 

Note: As per our Minimum Standards, if the Home Loan On-Boarding Application form is not provided in the above scenario, the bank will raise an Application Exemption and the application will not progress until the outstanding requirement has been satisfied.
