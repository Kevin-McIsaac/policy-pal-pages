* Customers switching to a Fixed Rate will not have access to Repayment Redraw during the Fixed Rate period. Therefore, any required amount must be withdrawn prior to the switch.

*** Customers do not have an automatic right to switch from a No Fee Variable Rate loan. However, customers can request a variation to the contract and switch to eligible loan types by submitting a completed Loan Switching Request Form and agreeing to the fees payable on the new loan type. Such requests will normally be accepted by the Bank.

^ The Bank will consider switching requests from No Fee Variable Rate home/investment home loan to these loan types.

  1. Subject to approval. Please contact your RM for these requests to be considered.

  2. Customers don't have an automatic right to switch from a No Fee Variable Rate, Base Variable rate (Economiser) and Base Variable Rate (Economiser) 3 Year Special to Extra Home Loan / Investment Home Loan. However, we will consider the switch. Please contact your RM for these requests to be considered.

  3. If a customer requests a re-assessment on their existing Extra Home Loan / Investment Home Loan margin, a minimum of $10,000 top up amount applies (the margin may increase or decrease and will change to the current advertised discount). Other requests to change discount are not permitted including a top up for less than $10,000.

  4. The new loan must have Principal and Interest repayments. Requests to switch from a Viridian Line of Credit to a Home Loan or Investment Home Loan with Interest Only payments is considered a switch to Interest Only and an assessment is required. See For Principal and Interest to Interest Only switches.

  5. Customers will only be able to switch the outstanding Viridian Line of Credit balance to an eligible Home Loan Product.
