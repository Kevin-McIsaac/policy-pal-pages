Note: You are only required to post hard copies of Executed Loan Documents to Mortgage Services. This includes the documents listed below:

 

All States

  • Australian Standard Transfer
  • Credit Assessment Summary
  • CCC Loan Schedule
  • Certificate of Title
  • Change of Name
  • Discharge of Mortgages
  • Guarantee/Extension of Guarantee
  • Letter/Notice of Variation
  • Loan Account Authority (previously known as the Consumer Loan Authority)
  • Mortgages & All Charging Documents (e.g. CBA Mortgage, Letter of Set Off)
  • Statutory Declarations
  • Transfers
  • Withdrawal of Caveat/Caveat
  • Discharge of Mortgage
  • Consent to Second/ Subsequent Mortgage

 

South Australia Only

  • FHOGS Applications (including related supporting documents)
