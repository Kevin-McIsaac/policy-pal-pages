Due to COVID19

CommBank’s relief options for Viridian Line of Credit customers impacted by Coronavirus

If your customer has a Viridian Line of Credit (VLOC) and is impacted by Coronavirus, we have the following support options:

Option 1: If their balance is under the limit: Stop making monthly interest payments on the VLOC until they exceed their limit. If there is an interest redirection set up, this will need to be cancelled by the Bank.

Option 2:If their balance is over the limit: Contact FAS for hardship support on 1300 720 814.

 

Eligibility Criteria

This product is available to customers who: test

  • Are living in Australia as:
  • An Australian citizen, permanent resident or specified temporary Australian resident; or
  • A New Zealand citizen; or
  • An overseas citizen co-borrowing with an Australian citizen or permanent resident; or
  • Are living and working overseas as an Australian citizen or permanent resident; or
  • Are an Australian non-trading family trust with a beneficiary and trustee which meet the eligibility criteria of an individual (as above); and
  • Provide sufficient security in accordance with CommBank’s credit assessment criteria, including:
  • Acceptable residential mortgage; or
  • Acceptable residential mortgage with supplementary cash held in an eligible CommBank account; and/or
  • Guarantor who supports the loan by providing additional security.

 

Customer objectives, financial situation and needs

This product is for customers who:

  • Want to access equity from a residential owner occupied or investment property via a revolving line of credit, with no set term or fixed repayments for the purpose of:
  • Assisting with:
  • Purchasing, renovating, or refinancing a residential owner occupied or investment property;
  • Purchase of land; or
  • Personal needs and personal investments (including consolidation of personal debt).
  • Meet CommBank’s credit assessment criteria; and
  • Have a reasonable plan for paying out the line of credit.

 

Key features

  • Access a revolving credit facility with a variable interest rate, which means repayments will vary based on changes to the interest rate.
  • A maximum credit limit of $150,000.
  • Have a secured pre-approved credit limit to ensure there is credit available without having to reapply for it.
  • Deposit money into the account and access this money by:
  • Keycard;
  • Debit Mastercard;
  • Platinum Debit Mastercard;
  • Direct Debit;
  • Chequebook;
  • Online banking including NetBank,the CommBank App, CommBiz and the CommBiz App;
  • Phone Banking;
  • Staff assisted channels e.g. in branch, over the phone, with a relationship manager or mobile banker and Australia Post Offices; and
  • ATMs.
  • Have the option to choose the repayment amount and frequency without a set loan term (but ensuring that the monthly interest, fees and charges on any credit drawn are paid).
  • Customers who pay an annual fee can access a Mortgage Advantage Package (which is subject to its own eligibility criteria), to receive a discount on their Viridian Line of Credit interest rate as well as waiver of specified fees.

 

 

Other Features and Benefits Product Attributes Repayment Options
  • Access funds up to agreed limit (subject to daily card limits where applicable)
  • Interest is only charged on funds used
  • Portability - the line of credit can move with the customer (conditions and fees apply).
  • Minimum Loan Amount - $20,000
  • Maximum Loan Amount - Viridian Line of Credit (VLOC) has a maximum credit limit of $150,000 for new loan applications and existing VLOCs may only be topped up to a maximum credit limit of $150,000.
  • Minimum/maximum Term - not applicable
  • There are no contracted minimum repayments, but customers must at least make a payment to cover monthly interest, fees and charges.

Note: Interest cannot be charged to a related account

 

Suitability

VLOC is aimed at customers who're comfortable with managing their own debt reduction program and consolidating their financial affairs. The ideal customer should:

  • Be a professional, self-employed person or executive.
  • Have a good cash flow (i.e. spare cash after any regular lending repayments and general living expenses).
  • Be financially aware (i.e. have detailed knowledge about the alternative finance options available in the market and the mechanics of how a revolving line of credit product works).
  • Be financially disciplined (i.e. be comfortable with the idea of managing a revolving line of credit product with no fixed repayments). Customers with a history of asset accumulation or debt reduction are likely to be financially disciplined.
  • Be interested in using a VLOC to invest in other types of assets, especially existing home loan customers with high equity in their properties.
  • Hold multiple loans or investment properties.
