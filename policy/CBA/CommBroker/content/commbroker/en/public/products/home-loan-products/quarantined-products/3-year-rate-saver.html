3 Year Special Rate Saver Home / Investment Home Loan - Quarantined September 2018

Back to Quarantined Home Loan Products

 

Features and Benefits Product Attributes Repayment Options
  • Discounted base variable rate that stays below the Rate Saver Base Variable Interest Rate (BVR) for 3 years.
  • At the end of the discounted period, the rate converts to the Bank's applicable BVR current at the time (ie. Rate Saver BVR) relevant to the customers loan purpose and repayment type; or the customer can switch to another interest rate option.
  • Unlimited Special repayments (over repayments)
  • Repayment Holiday using Special repayments. This option allows customers to suspend loan repayments for nominated periods of 3 to 12 months, offering flexibility during lifestyle changes such as a career break, pregnancy or parental leave.
  • Repayment Redraw*
  • Interest calculated daily, charged monthly so the customer benefits immediately from every repayment they make.
  • Portability - the home loan can move with the customer (conditions and fees apply).
  • Switch to a fixed interest rate at any time.
  • Card access available for Home / Investment Home Loans in single names or where there is more than one name the Method of Operation is 'Either to Operate'.
  • Statement frequency 6 monthly or on request (a fee applies for requested statements).
  • Loan Increase - borrow additional funds on an existing loan quickly and easily, without taking out a separate loan (an Establishment Fee may apply). This is allowed on introductory rate loans if the increase is more than 50% of the total new loan. Benefits customers who are planning renovations, want to consolidate debts or just need extra cash. 
  • Minimum Loan Amount - $10,000
  • Maximum Loan Amount - no maximum
  • Minimum Term - 1 year
  • Maximum Term - 30 years (subject to expected retirement age) conditions apply to interest only - see "Repayment Options"
  • Monthly, fortnightly and weekly repayment options are available (interest only is monthly).
  • Payment options of principle and interest and interest only (subject to a maximum of 10 years) are available.
  • Interest only - make interest only payments for a period of 1 to 5 years per IO term with a maximum of 5 IO years in total for Home Loans and 1 to 5 years per IO term with a maximum of 10 IO years in total for Investment Home Loans.
  • Additional repayments of any amount can be made without incurring an Early Repayment Adjustment (ERA).

 

* minimum $1000 over the counter at any Commonwealth Bank branch, or no minimum electronically, both with a $0 redraw fee. Note - If Interest Only Option is chosen, Repayment Redraw is not available for the life of the loan. If customers subsequently want the Repayment Redraw option they must switch to a Standard Variable Rate.
