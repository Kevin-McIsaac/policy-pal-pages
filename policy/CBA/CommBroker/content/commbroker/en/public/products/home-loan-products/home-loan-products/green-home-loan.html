CommBank Green Loan/ Green Investment Loan

Back to Home Loan Products

Features and Benefits Product Attributes Repayment Options
  • For the installation of eligible small-scale renewables such as solar panels, battery packs or electric vehicle charging stations, switching from gas to electric cooking equipment, smart connected home products, energy efficient window treatments, switching from non LED to LED lighting & virtual power plants.
  • Low rate of 3.99% fixed for 10 years
  • No establishment or monthly loan servicing fees
  • Flexibility to make additional repayments with no early repayment charges
  • Minimum Loan Amount - $5,000
  • Maximum Loan Amount - $30,000
  • Loan Term - 10 years
  • No Early Repayment Adjustment or administrative fees
  • Monthly, fortnightly and weekly repayment options are available
  • Principal and interest only
  • Interest payments will be charged while loan is progressively drawn, and principle and interest repayments will commence once the loan is fully funded

 

  • Customers must have an eligible existing Commonwealth Bank home loan to use as security for the Green Loan
  • Customers can apply by contacting our Green Loans Direct Lending Team on 1800 965 047
  • Existing Broker origination relationship will not be impacted by Green loan applications
  • Brokers are unable to originate Green Loans
