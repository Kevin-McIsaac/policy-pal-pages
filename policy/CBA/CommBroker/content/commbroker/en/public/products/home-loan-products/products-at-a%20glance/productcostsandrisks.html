To meet CommBanks Responsible Lending obligations under the National Consumer Credit Protection Act requirements and the Product Design and Distribution Obligations, CommBank requires you to ensure that the customer understands all of the features costs and risks of the selected CommBank product. Refer National Consumer Credit Protection Act.
 

When an application is submitted to the CommBank, you are required to:


            • Explain the features, costs and risks of the selected CommBank product to the customer
            • Ensure that the customer understand and accepts the features, costs and risks.
