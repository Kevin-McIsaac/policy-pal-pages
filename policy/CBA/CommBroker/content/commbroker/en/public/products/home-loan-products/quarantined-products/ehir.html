Extra Home Loan / Investment Home Loan (Introductory Variable Rate) - Quarantined October 2020

Back to Extra Home Loan

Features and Benefits Product Attributes Repayment Options
  • Repayment Redraw*
  • Interest calculated daily, charged monthly so the customer benefits immediately from every repayment they make.
  • Discount on variable interest rate**
  • Save on interest with a higher discounted variable interest rate for the introductory period, and at the end of the introductory period, a lower discount for the remaining life of the loan
  • Portability - the home loan can move with the customer (conditions and fees apply)
  • Switch to a fixed interest rate at any time
  • Card access is available for Home / Investment Home Loans***
  • Statement frequency 6 monthly or on request (a fee applies for requested statements)
  • Minimum Loan Amount - $150,000
  • Maximum Loan Amount - no maximum
  • Minimum Term - 1 year
  • Maximum Term - 30 years (subject to expected retirement age)
  • Monthly, fortnightly and weekly repayment options are available (interest only is monthly).
  • Payment options of principal and interest and interest only available.
  • Interest only - make interest only payments for a maximum period of 10 years for Investment Home loans and 5 years of Owner-Occupied Home loans, as long as there is a minimum of five years remaining on the Contracted Loan Term.
  • Additional repayments of any amount can be made without incurring an Early Repayment Adjustment (ERA).

 

* (Minimum redraw amounts and fee amounts depending on the redraw method used i.e. over the counter at any Commonwealth Bank branch: $1,000 with a $0 redraw fee, or electronically no minimum with a $0 redraw fee).

** Product discount margin off the applicable variable interest rate relevant to the customers loan purpose and repayment type available at the time loan documents are issued.

***In single names or where there is more than one name and the method of operation is 'Either to Operate'.
