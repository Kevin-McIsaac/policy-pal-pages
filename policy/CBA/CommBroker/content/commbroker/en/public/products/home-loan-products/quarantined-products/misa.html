Back to Quarantined Home Loan Products

Features and Benefits

 

A MISA is a feature of eligible home loan products and is automatically established and linked to the loan account at the time of funding, with a method of operation of "Either to Operate". The customer can change the method of operation by contacting the Bank.

If your customer switches or splits their loan, their MISA may be closed and can't be reactivated or reopened. Customers won't be able to open a new MISA feature on their new loan(s).

 

The MISA facility is subject to the terms and conditions as detailed in Section 4 of the Consumer Mortgage Lending Products booklet.

Partial Offset means that a reduced interest rate (currently 1.5% p.a. less than the contracted fixed interest rate) is charged on the part of the home loan account equal to the balance of the MISA.
