1. About this policy

1.1 Why this policy?

The Bank requires acceptable building insurance cover on a replacement and reinstatement basis over the insurable collateral taken as security to secure the loan.  
Insurance cover will protect the value of the secured asset in the event that the Bank is required to rely upon it.
Adequate insurance cover is to be maintained for the duration of the loan.
 

2. Rules you must apply

2.1 Insurance policy requirements  

2.1.1 When do building insurance policy need to be taken up

For completed property (i.e. property that is not under construction)
Building insurance policies on a replacement and reinstatement basis for at least the amount of cover specified in the loan offer is to be received prior to settlement. This is undertaken by the Bank’s mortgage preparers. Cover Notes and Brokers letters are not acceptable.
For property under construction
Building insurance policies on a replacement and reinstatement basis for at least the amount of cover specified in the loan offer is to be received prior to final progress payment being released. This is undertaken by the Bank’s mortgage preparers. Cover Notes and Brokers letters are not acceptable.
Owner Builders
Owner builders are to take out builders all risk insurance covering the building work (during construction).
Kit Homes/Transportable Homes
Borrowers are to take out all risk insurance during periods of manufacture (Kit), transport/delivery (Kit and Transportable), and construction.
In each of the above:
  • The insurance cover is to cover the whole period until practical completion (lock up), and
  • Borrowers are to provide the bank with a copy of the insurance policy or, a certificate of currency, prior to settlement (first advance)
Building insurance requirement is not needed for:
  • Strata or company title securities;
  • Vacant land securities where the Bank is relying solely on the (vacant) land value

2.1.2 Roles and Responsibilities


Whilst minimum property insurance is a condition of the loan, you cannot ask a borrower to take insurance with a particular company. The borrowers have the right to select their own insurer.
Failure to have a current or renew building insurance policy during the loan contract term is a breach under the terms of the contract and mortgage by borrower/ mortgagor.
 

2.1.3 Minimum building cover

Minimum cover is to include protection in the events of damage caused through fire, explosion, earthquake, storm and tempest with flood cover required in flood prone areas.
Minimum amount is to be quoted in the loan offer for each security property. The minimum insurance amount may be obtained from short form valuation report. Under no circumstances are loan offers to be issued showing minimum insurance amount as $0.00 except where building insurance requirement is not needed .

 

2.1.4 Insurance policy details

As a minimum, the insurance policy is to detail the following information:
  • The owner of the asset / security (upon settlement / drawdown);
  • Policy number
  • Insurance company
  • Expiry date of the policy
  • Adequate description of the location of the asset / security (e.g. address, title details)
  • Amount of cover, on a replacement and reinstatement basis, for at least the amount specified in the loan offer.
