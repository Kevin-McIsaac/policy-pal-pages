As Fixed Rates are determined at settlement of your loan, our Guaranteed Rate Fee (Rate Lock) removes this uncertainty by fixing the rate at application for up to 90 days to protect you from unforeseen increases before loan settlement.
The minimum Guaranteed Rate Fee is $600 or 0.15% (whichever is higher) per fixed loan, which is charged to the loan at settlement.
Note: Pre-approval loans must be converted to a full application prior to the Guaranteed Rate Lock commencing.

Common Questions

All Suncorp Bank loans, including Fixed Rates, can change prior to a loan being funded (Settlement) after which time the rate will be fixed for the Fixed Interest Period. This settlement process means that the interest rate quoted at the time of application can change at any time prior to the settlement of the loan, depending on market forces. The Guaranteed Rate Fee (GRF) secures a fixed interest rate from the day the GRF was requested and guarantees the fixed interest rate for a further 90 days from the date of final approval. The net fixed rate that was secured by the GRF during the application process will be the net fixed rate applied at settlement. The Guaranteed Rate Fee secures a ceiling rate. If the base rate drops at settlement, the lower rate applies. Refer to the Lending Fees and Charges brochure.

To ensure GRF is correctly applied; in your ApplyOnline loan submission, Select Yes for both prompts “Is this feature required?” and “Do you wish to guarantee the fixed interest rate?”. This section is located in the product features section on the Loan Details tab in Apply Online (AOL).

It is a mandatory requirement to discuss and explain GRF to a customer. Refer customers to the disclosure on the home loan application form
