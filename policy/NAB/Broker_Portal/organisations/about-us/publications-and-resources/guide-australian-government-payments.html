More information on our payments

Read about who can get a payment, how to apply and how to manage it.

Families

Payments for families include:

  • Additional Child Care Subsidy
  • Child Care Subsidy
  • Double Orphan Pension
  • Family Tax Benefit
  • Newborn Supplement and Newborn Upfront Payment
  • Parental Leave Pay
  • Parenting Payment
  • Single Income Family Supplement
  • Stillborn Baby Payment.

Older Australians

Payments for older Australians include:

  • Age Pension
  • Home Equity Access Scheme.

People with disability

Payments for people with disability include:

  • Disability Support Pension
  • Essential Medical Equipment Payment
  • Mobility Allowance
  • Youth Disability Supplement.

Carers

Payments for carers include:

  • Carer Allowance
  • Carer Payment
  • Carer Supplement
  • Child Disability Assistance Payment.

Youth and students

Payments for students and Australian apprentices include:

  • ABSTUDY
  • Assistance for Isolated Children Scheme
  • Austudy
  • Pensioner Education Supplement
  • Tertiary Access Payment
  • Youth Allowance for students and apprentices.

Job seekers

Payments for job seekers include:

  • JobSeeker Payment
  • Youth Allowance for job seekers.

Farm support

Farm Household Allowance is the main farm support payment.

Special circumstances

Special circumstances payments include:

  • Crisis Payment
  • Special Benefit.

Supplementary payments

Supplementary payments include:

  • Energy Supplement
  • Pension Supplement
  • Pharmaceutical Allowance
  • Remote Area Allowance
  • Rent Assistance
  • Telephone Allowance
  • Utilities Allowance.

Concession cards

Concession cards include:

  • Commonwealth Seniors Health Card
  • Health Care Card
  • Pensioner Concession Card.
