What you need to do

  • Enter the applicant's details into each section, including the loan term required.
  • Once complete, include a copy of the serviceability calculation with the supporting documents for your client’s application.

How it works

The Calculator is based on:

  • the current NAB servicing rate
  • the loan to value ratio (LVR)
  • the Net Disposable Income (NDI)
  • customer stated living expenses
  • estimated loan repayment amounts.

These rates and ratios (and how thay are applied) may be changed at any time.

Conditions of use

The Serviceability Calculator is not a loan offer, nor is it an indication of our intention to formally approve the loan application, or a prediction of the borrower’s actual ability to service a loan. It merely indicates the borrower’s compliance with our loan serviceability guidelines based on the information you have entered. 

All applications are subject to our full Credit Guidelines and approval process. The outcome of this assessment is to be used as a guide only as to whether or not your applicant’s criteria meets our standards for an initial "Approval in Principle". 

Ultimately, loan applications may be manually reviewed to take into account the various criteria in the loan application. We may, in our discretion, decline an application even though it appears to meet our serviceability criteria.
