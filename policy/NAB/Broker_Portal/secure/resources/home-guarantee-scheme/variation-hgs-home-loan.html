To support customers with their Scheme-backed loan with NAB, customers can make amendments (loan modifications and variations) to their home loan if permitted under the Scheme.

For all HGS home loan amendments (modifications and variations):

  • the principal limit or loan term must not increase (unless experiencing financial hardship and under active management by NAB Assist)
  • the property/title reference must not change
  • the borrower/s must not change (apart from where a borrower is removed from the home loan title and guarantee certificate), the repayment type must remain P&I (unless construction loan)
  • guarantee must not be transferred to another security.

Loan modifications

Modifications are to be completed via the NAB Request to Change my home loan, opens in a new window form.

Loan variations

Loan variations permitted under the Scheme will be submitted through a Variation Application and verified/assessed versus a specific set of verification requirements.

Most loan variations do not require a new Guarantee Certificate to be generated – apart from where a borrower is removed from the home loan and title as the Guarantee Certificate must also be amended with only one borrower listed.
Permitted Loan VariationsNon-Permitted Loan Variations
Permitted Loan Variations
  • Loan term reduction
  • Loan limit reduction
  • Removal of a borrower from an existing NAB HGS home loan (2 to 1)
  • IO Extension (if initial 12-month term has expired – Max IO Term is 24 months). Refer to important note in next section.
Non-Permitted Loan Variations
  • Interest type change from Principal & Interest to Interest Only (in non-hardship)
  • Interest Only extension (in non-hardship excluding construction)
  • Principal limit increase
  • Loan term extension
  • Security swaps (as the Government Guarantee is available only for the customer’s first home security, not subsequent property securities).
  • Any type of Loan consolidation request
  • Adding a new Guarantor

1. Prepare your customer’s variation application by choosing the applicable loan variation and check the below is completed correctly:

  • Customer’s details are accurate in the ‘Applicants’ tab and are the same as the customer’s details on the HGS home loan account.
  • Existing home loan account is linked to the customer’s HGS home loan account.
  • Loan term and loan amount in the ‘Loan Details’ tab does not exceed the loan term and amount on the customer’s HGS home loan account.
  • Residential property linked in the ‘Security’ tab is the same as the customer’s details on the HGS home loan account.
  • Primary purpose and principal residence are ‘Owner Occupied’ and ‘Principal Residence’.
  • Repayment type remains the same as the customer’s repayment type on the HGS home loan account.

Important: for permitted interest only extensions (construction) under the Home Guarantee Scheme, please ensure you are flagging the application as backed by the Home Guarantee Scheme and creating a new security under the ‘Security’ tab depending on guarantee type:

  • For First Home/New Home Guarantee backed loans, select ‘Government Guarantee – First Home’ OR for Family Home Guarantee backed loans, select ‘Government Guarantee – Family Home’ and enter the respective Scheme ID under ‘Guarantee Scheme ID’.

2. Add an MIS note (mandatory):
‘Application is for a permitted HGS Loan Variation - [LOAN TERM REDUCTION / REMOVAL OF A BORROWER FROM A JOINT FHLDS HOME LOAN (2 to 1) / IO EXTENSION FOR CONSTRUCTION. Confirmed the variation is permitted under the Scheme using [INFORMATION SOURCE].’

Continue to additional steps if the variation is for removal of a borrower (2 to 1):

3. Obtain customer consent via the ‘Personal Consent’ form to collect information for purposes of submitting a ‘Refinancing Enquiry’ to Housing Australia and processing their ‘Refinance Scheme Application’.

4. Provide to your customer/s the HGS Refinance Statutory Declaration (PDF, 136KB), opens in a new window form and ask them to complete, sign with an approved witness. Upload the completed form in the ‘Supporting Docs’ tab under ‘Other’.

5. Add a MIS note informing which customer is to be removed from the HGS loan account and property title.

Important: you must add an MIS note informing the variation is for a HGS loan to fast track the application.

Once your customer’s application has been verified, assessed, and checked, the NAB teams will execute the change on the home loan account and generate a new Guarantee Certificate (if required).
