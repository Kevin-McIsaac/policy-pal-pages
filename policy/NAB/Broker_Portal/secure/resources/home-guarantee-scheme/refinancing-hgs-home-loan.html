Customers looking to refinance their Government Guarantee-backed loan will need to satisfy the ‘Refinancing Criteria’ before their existing Government Guarantee can be confirmed with Housing Australia.

Applicants looking to refinance their Scheme-backed loan to NAB are subject to strict customer and loan eligibility criteria. Check and confirm your customer’s eligibility for refinance before proceeding to put through an application.

Customers eligible to take the benefit of the Government Guarantee for refinanced loans must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age, and an Australian citizen or permanent resident.
  • Be the same applicant/s as the Government Guarantee-backed loan to be refinanced.
    • (For jointly held lending) If the applicants wish to refinance solely into either one of the two applicants’ names, the application will need to be put through as a ‘Loan Variation’ application. Refer to ‘Loan Amendments’<link to HGS loan variation page>.
  • Be the same loan term and loan amount as the Government Guarantee-backed loan to be refinanced.
  • Be refinancing the same residential property mortgaged under the Government Guarantee-backed loan to be refinanced.
  • Have a home loan backed by the Government Guarantee with another participating lender with no claims made on the Guarantee until (and including) loan settlement date.
  • Intend to live in the property for duration the lending remains guaranteed under the Scheme.
  • Be owner occupier/s and sole registered owner/s of the residential property to be refinanced.
  • Take out a loan with eligible repayment type:
    • Principal and interest with maximum 30-year loan term; or
    • Interest only for maximum 24-month construction period for construction loans (fixed price building contracts only for internal refinance from two borrowers to one).

The ‘New Loans’ eligibility criteria for HGS loans e.g., Housing Australia Property Price Thresholds, customer’s income threshold, etc. will not apply for the refinancing of Scheme-backed loans. Only the above refinancing criteria will apply.

  1. Obtain customer consent via the Personal Consent Form to collect information for purposes of submitting a ‘Refinancing Enquiry’ to Housing Australia and processing their ‘Refinance Scheme Application’.
  2. Provide to your customer/s the ‘HGS Refinance Statutory Declaration’ form below and ask them to complete, sign with an approved witness. Upload the completed form in the ’Supporting Docs’ tab under ‘Other’.
    • Home Guarantee Scheme Refinance Statutory Declaration Form (PDF, 136KB), opens in a new window
  3. Complete the application and check all sections have been completed correctly:
    • Citizenship for each applicant: Australian or Australian permanent resident
    • Date of birth for each applicant: 18 years or older
    • Marital status: Any
    • Loan purpose/ABS Code: 133 - Re-finance of existing of home loans - Refinancing of housing loans of other financial institutions
    • Repayment type: P&I if purchasing an existing dwelling or IO for construction loans with progressive drawdowns with a max. 24-month IO term
    • LVR equal to 80.00%
    • HGS flag present in ‘Summary’ tab ('Yes' option must be selected for ‘Are the applicant(s) applying for the Government Guarantee Scheme?’)
    • Government Guarantee added as security type (in addition to the property being purchased):
      • For FHBG, NHG and RFHBG applications: ‘Government Guarantee - First Home Guarantee’
      • For FHG applications: ‘Government Guarantee - Family Home Guarantee’
    • Government Guarantee Value calculated to reduce LVR to equal 80.00%
    • Government Guarantee Scheme ID: 
      • If the customers current Home Loan with the outgoing lender is backed by the First Home Guarantee (FHBG):
        • Enter dummy ID 0000-00-00000NAB0 into field (this will be updated with the confirmed Scheme ID from Housing Australia)
      • If the customers current Home Loan with the outgoing lender is backed by the Family Home Guarantee (FHG):
        • Enter dummy ID 5555-55-55555NAB5 into field (this will be updated with the confirmed Scheme ID from Housing Australia)
      • If the customers current Home Loan with the outgoing lender is backed by the Regional First Home Buyer Guarantee (RFHBG):
        • Enter dummy ID 7777-77-77777NAB7 into field (this will be updated with the confirmed Scheme ID from Housing Australia)
      • If the customers current Home Loan with the outgoing lender is backed by the New Home Guarantee (NHG):
        • Enter dummy ID 9999-99-99999NAB9 into field (this will be updated with the confirmed Scheme ID from Housing Australia)
  4. Submit the application for conditional approval. NAB will submit a ‘Refinancing Enquiry’ to Housing Australia to confirm your customer’s existing Government Guarantee and refinancing loan parameters (e.g., maximum loan term, loan limit, etc.). If approved the team will notify you of the next steps.
  5. Notify your customer/s of the Scheme Application ID and next steps.
  6. Upload all required HGS documents into the application once obtained. See ‘Confirm customer eligibility for refinance’ above & ‘Key Documents & Evidence’.

Prior to submitting for unconditional approval, ensure that you have uploaded the following required documents:

  • Personal consent form sourced from ApplyOnline.
  • HGS Refinance Statutory Declaration Form
  • Document evidence for Australian citizen/permanent resident and age.

Refer to ‘Customer eligibility & supporting documents (ready for unconditional approval)’ for detailed documentation requirements.

The NAB Credit teams will verify and assess the application and the customers’ refinance eligibility to complete the final assessment.

Once completed, the team will notify you of the outcome.

Assist your customer/s during settlement stage with any questions.

Please notify the Broker HGS Support team (HGSBrokerSupport@nab.com.au) of the settlement outcome via email including the confirmed settlement day. The Broker HGS Support team will be in touch.

If your customers settlement date is delayed for any reason whatsoever, please notify the Broker HGS Support team (HGSBrokerSupport@nab.com.au) of the new expected settlement date.
