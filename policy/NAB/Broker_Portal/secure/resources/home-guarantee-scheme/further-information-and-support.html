Products

Choice Package1, Base Variable, and Tailored Home Loan Products

Note: Land only, Owner Builder, Defence Construction, and Off-the-plan (OTP) contracts prior to or in the course of construction for standard HGS loans are excluded.

Important information: 1NAB Choice Package Home Loan no longer available for sale

The NAB Choice Package Home Loan is no longer available for sale from 23 February 2023 to NAB Broker customers unless they have an existing (and ongoing) NAB Choice Package established before that date.

Repayment Type

Principal & Interest (P&I) repayments

Note: Interest Only (IO) repayments available only on construction loans for the period up to build completion date i.e., max. 24 months.

Construction Products

The following construction loans with progressive drawdowns are eligible:

  • ‘House and Land Package’ with progressive drawdowns (contract/s from same vendor)
    • Split contracts are not acceptable unless from nominated large scale builders accepted by NAB as per policy i.e., Stocklands, Mirvac, Metricon.
  • ‘Land and separate Contract to Build a Home’ with progressive drawdowns (contracts from different vendors with both land and build contracts already held by the customer)

Construction loans are only accepted if the following criteria is satisfied:

  • Registered builders only (no owner-builder)
  • Fixed price contract only
  • Maximum 24 months build time
  • Progressive drawdowns
  • Land subdivided and titled.

First Home/Family Home Guarantee:

Off-the-plan purchases are available but only for properties where:

  • Construction is completed 
  • Land is subdivided and child titled (not parent titled)
  • Occupancy certificate has been issued
  • A valuation is ready to be conducted.

New Home Guarantee:

Off-the-plan arrangements where construction is in progress or yet to start are available however are subject to specific requirements.

  • Customers will need to submit a conditional approval application to reserve a Scheme Place under the New Home Guarantee.
  • After your customer signs a contract and enters into an Off-the-plan arrangement, their Scheme place will be extended for ~24 or ~36 months depending on whether build has commenced. Once nearing build completion, your customer will need to submit a new Home Guarantee application to request for finance.
  • Note: your customer is not guaranteed to receive finance with NAB if they reserve a Scheme place due to the potential change in valuation or their future financial circumstances.
  • Land only, Owner Builder and Off-the-plan contracts prior to or in course of construction (for standard Home Guarantee loans) are excluded.
