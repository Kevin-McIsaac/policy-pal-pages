Please notify the Broker HGS Support team (HGSBrokerSupport@nab.com.au) of the settlement outcome via email including the confirmed settlement day. The Broker HGS Support team will record the settlement and ensure that your customer’s Guarantee takes effect.

If your customers’ settlement date is delayed, please notify the Broker HGS Support team (HGSBrokerSupport@nab.com.au) of the new expected settlement date.

It is crucial that settlement takes place on or before the Guarantee Certificate expiry date.

The Guarantee Certificate expiry date is typically valid for 100 days from the date unconditional/formal approval is issued by NAB.
