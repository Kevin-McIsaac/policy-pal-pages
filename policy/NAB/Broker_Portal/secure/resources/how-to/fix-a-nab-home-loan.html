Introduction

Customers can now fix and re-fix their interest rate on an eligible NAB home loan straight from the NAB mobile app. The request will be processed without the need for any manual intervention.

Please see below for instructions on how your customer can fix or re-fix their NAB home loan via the NAB mobile app:

  1. Log in to the NAB app.
  2. Tap the home loan account they would like to fix on the Accounts screen.
  3. In the Account Details screen, tap on the ‘Manage’ button.
  4. In the Manage your loan screen, if the customer is eligible, they will see the option to “Fix your interest rate”. They can tap on this option to continue. If they do not see the option, refer to the eligibility criteria below.
  5. Customer must read the key benefits and trade off's and if comfortable, tap on Yes, let’s start.
  6. Select a fixed term, review the changes to repayments, read the important information and tap on Review Changes.
  7. In the Review your changes screen, the customer can compare their new loan repayments to their current repayments.
  8. Tap on Review loan terms to continue.
  9. In the Confirm my changes screen, customers must read the important information and understand the economic costs associated with breaking their fixed loan.
  10. Ensure the customer understands this before they proceed.
  11. Customers must tick the two checkboxes stating that they understand the potential changes and understand the break costs before they tap “Agree and submit”.
  12. In the Success screen, the customer will see when the change goes through. It will show them when the next minimum repayment will be made and will notify them that a confirmation letter will be sent in the mail.

Important:

In certain scenarios the customer will see a Changes processing screen which will indicate that the request has been received and will be processed in 1 – 2 business days. The customer will be notified via SMS once the change has been processed successfully.

For eligible home loans with two applicants, if one applicant initiates a request to fix the interest rate on their home loan via the NAB app, then the second applicant will need to approve the request via the NAB app for the same to be processed.

  1. Log in to the NAB app.
  2. Tap the home loan account they would like to fix on the Accounts screen.
  3. In the Account Details screen, tap on the ‘Manage’ button.
  4. In the Manage your loan screen, if the customer is eligible, they will see the option to “Fix your interest rate”. They can tap on this option to continue. If they do not see the option, refer to the eligibility criteria below.
  5. Customer must read the key benefits and trade off's and if comfortable, tap on Yes, let’s start.
  6. Select a fixed term, review the changes to repayments, read the important information and tap on Review Changes.
  7. In the Review your changes screen, the customer can compare their new loan repayments to their current repayments.
  8. Tap on Review loan terms to continue.
  9. In the Confirm my changes screen, customers must read the important information and understand the economic costs associated with breaking their fixed loan.
  10. Ensure the customer understands this before they proceed.
  11. Customers must tick the two checkboxes stating that they understand the potential changes and understand the break costs before they tap “Agree and submit”.
  12. Once completed, they will see a Changes pending screen where the second applicant will need to log in to their own NAB app and approve or decline the request.
  13. The second applicant will receive an SMS and then need to log in to their own NAB app with their own NAB ID and tap onto the home loan in question.
  14. In the Account Details screen, tap on the ‘Manage’ button.
  15. In the Manage your loan screen, if the customer is eligible, they will see the option “Recent changes awaiting your approval”. They can tap on this option to continue.
  16. They will then be presented with the same information as the first customer and will have the option to either Approve or Decline the request.
  17. If they tap “Approve”, they will see a Changes processing screen which will indicate that the request has been received and will be processed in 1 – 2 business days. Customers will be notified via SMS once the change has been processed successfully. If they tap “Decline”, the change will be rejected and not occur.

Important:

The second applicant will have 48 hours to approve or reject the change, else the request will expire, and they will have to start again.

Customers who are eligible to re-fix will see an option in their NAB App up to 45 days prior to expiry

  1. Tap on the home loan you would like to make changes to.
  2. In the home loan screen, tap the ‘Manage’ button.
  3. In the Manage your loan screen, the customer will see a feature message which notifies them of their fixed rate expiring and the options available.
  • The customer can choose to refix their home loan once it expires. The instructions are exactly the same as “Fix my home loan” so refer to that if guidance is needed
  • If the customer does not to anything, their loan will revert to a variable rate upon expiry

Note: If this is a joint home loan, the instructions will be the same except this will follow the Multi Approval pathway. The instructions for this are as above. 

Alternatively, switching a NAB Home Loan fixed interest rate can be requested by completing the request to change my home loan.

If your customer wants to fix a portion of their existing loan, then you will need to submit a simple loan split request. More information about loan splits.
