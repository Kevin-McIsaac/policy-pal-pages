How do i submit a variation for a NAB home loan?

Variations that do not require credit assessment can be initiated digitally via the Request to Change Form. For any assistance in filling out the form, please refer to the Quick reference guide, opens in a new window or FAQs, opens in a new window or contact our Broker Response Centre on 1300 622 276. Please remember to complete & sign the form before emailing it to loanmods@nab.com.au.

The following variations can be completed via the Request to Change form:

  • Fix/Re-fix my home loan
  • Cancel my fixed rate period - switch to a variable rate
  • Change of loan purpose
  • Reduce my loan limit
  • Change my interest only repayments to principal and interest repayments
  • Loan split
  • Change to a different product
  • Add or remove NAB offset arrangement
  • Add or remove NAB Choice Package1 (Adding NAB Choice Package only available to customer with existing (and ongoing) NAB Choice Package)

Eligible single and joint home loan customers can also fix and re-fix their home loan in the NAB App. Find out more.

A customer could request a Discharge of their Mortgage and release of their documents if they are:

  • selling up
  • refinancing
  • have paid out their loan and simply want their documents back.

You can complete the NAB Discharge Authority Form, opens in a new window  and send it to us as outlined on the form.

To submit a simple loan split where there is no limit increase or any credit assessment required, click here

For all other variations you must either:

  1. Submit an application directly from the site using our Broker Centre platform.
  2. Submit the loan application via your aggregator software.
