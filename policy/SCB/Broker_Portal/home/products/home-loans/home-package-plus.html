Home Package Plus

A package that combines an eligible home loan, Everyday Options transaction account and other Suncorp Bank products in a single package for a low annual fee.

How it works

Choose from eligible Home Loans:

Standard Variable Rate

Fixed Rate

Combine borrowings of $150,000 or more with an Everyday Options bank account to get a discounted interest rate on the loan and pay $0 loan establishment fee.

OPTIONAL EXTRAS

Save with offers on other financial product and services

Eligible Credit Cards2

Pay no Annual Card fee on new Suncorp Bank Platinum or Standard Credit Cards.

More info: Credit Cards

Please note, introductory credit card offers are not available in conjunction with a Home Package Plus package. Should you choose to take up an introductory offer an Annual Card fee will apply.

Please refer to the Home Package Plus Terms and Conditions booklet for the full terms and conditions. This should be read in conjunction with the Lending Fees and Charges booklet.
