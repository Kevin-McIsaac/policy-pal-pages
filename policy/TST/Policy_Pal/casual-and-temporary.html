SECTION 1: POLICY DEFINITION AND EMPLOYMENT TYPES

Definition

  • Policy covers casual and temporary employees
  • Employees do not get paid sick or annual leave
  • Employees have non-guaranteed and varying hours of work
  • 100% of income can be used for servicing

Employment Categories

  1. Same Employer Category:
    • Casually and/or temporarily employed with same employer
    • Minimum period: 6 months
  2. Same Industry/Role Category:
    • Casually and/or temporarily employed in same industry/role
    • Can be different employers
    • Minimum period: 6 months
    • Definition of "same":
      • Similar product/service (e.g., Coles and Woolworths)
      • Similar role type (e.g., IT Business Analyst at CBA and IT Business Analyst at Woolworths)
  3. Exclusions:
    • Not applicable to seasonal workers with income only during season
    • Seasonal workers must refer to PAYG -- Seasonal income verification requirements

SECTION 2: VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

Documentation Options for Same Employer

  1. Option One:
    • Six months' salary credits to account AND
    • Either:
      • One payslip (<six months YTD income) OR
      • Employer letter with:
        • Working hours/rate
        • Employment length
        • YTD income breakdown
  2. Option Two:
    • One payslip showing >six months YTD income
  3. Option Three:
    • One payslip (<six months YTD income) AND
    • One of:
      • PAYG payment summary
      • 'Tax ready' Income Statement
      • Tax return
      • Final YTD payslip from previous financial year
  4. Option Four:
    • Historical document:
      • PAYG payment summary OR
      • 'Tax ready' Income Statement OR
      • Tax return OR
      • Final YTD payslip from previous year
    • AND employer letter with:
      • Current work arrangement
      • Employment length
      • YTD income breakdown

Documentation Options for Same Industry/Role

  • Six months' salary credits AND
  • Either:
    • Current payslip OR
    • Employer letter
  • Must document industry/role continuity in application comments

SECTION 3: CALCULATION METHODS

Same Employer Calculations

  1. Six Months' Average Method:
    • Total six months' salary credits
    • Divide by expected pay periods
    • Include non-pay periods
    • Convert to annual gross income
  2. YTD Method:
    • Annualize YTD amount from payslip
    • Use lower of:
      • Annualized YTD
      • Historical documentation amount

Same Industry/Role Calculations

  1. Calculate six-month average:
    • Total salary credits
    • Divide by pay periods
    • Convert to annual amount
  2. Calculate current rate:
    • Annualize current payslip/letter amount
  3. Use lower of:
    • Six-month average
    • Current annualized amount

SECTION 4: VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS AND NOTES

Bank Statement Requirements

  • Must be from salary-receiving bank
  • If internet banking lacks full details:
    • Provide old statement
    • Verify account ownership

Employment Timing

  • Starting after July 1 current year:
    • Verify start date
    • Provide evidence

Special Considerations

  1. Tax Offset Cases:
    • If tax offset withholding declaration exists:
      • Cannot use salary credits
      • Must use alternative verification
  2. Overtime Treatment:
    • Casual employees:
      • Include overtime in calculations
      • No separate overtime calculation needed
  3. Pay Frequency:
    • Monitor changes between employers
    • Adjust calculations accordingly
  4. Multiple Employers:
    • Must be same industry/role
    • Document in application
    • Track employment continuity
  5. Documentation Requirements:
    • Include industry/role consistency
    • Document in application comments
    • Maintain evidence chain
  6. Input Requirements:
    • Use annualized amounts
    • Document calculation method
    • Include supporting evidence
